## Instructions

Raise a Corrective Action Record (CAR) for every:

- Major or minor nonconformity identified during internal or external audits
- Security incident where a systemic improvement is required
- Observation escalated to a corrective action
- Management review decision requiring a formal action

CARs are referenced in TPL-03 Management Review Minutes and tracked in ISMS-12 Audit Programme. Assign a sequential ID using the format **CAR-YYYY-NNN** (e.g., CAR-2026-001).
