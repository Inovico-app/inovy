## Instructions

Record every security-related training activity for all Inovy employees, contractors, and relevant third parties. Training is required for ISO 27001:2022 Clause 7.2 (Competence) and 7.3 (Awareness), and Annex A control A.6.3 (Information security awareness, education and training).

Inovy's training completion target: **100% of all staff within 30 days of joining** (onboarding) and **100% of all staff by 31 December each year** (annual refresh). This is tracked as KPI-03 in TPL-09.
