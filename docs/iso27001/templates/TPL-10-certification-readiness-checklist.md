## Instructions

Work through this checklist in sequence. Complete Stage 1 readiness before contacting your certification body to schedule the audit. Stage 2 readiness should be confirmed 2 weeks before the Stage 2 audit date.

**Status Key:**

- **Complete** — Fully done; evidence available
- **In Progress** — Partially done; target date set
- **Not Started** — Not yet begun
- **N/A** — Not applicable to Inovy's scope

## Pre-Audit Preparation Checklist

_Complete this in the 2 weeks before each audit (Stage 1 or Stage 2)._

### Documentation Preparation

- [ ] All documents are version-controlled and show current approval date
- [ ] Document index / master list is up to date
- [ ] Evidence files are organised and accessible (shared drive, Notion, or equivalent)
- [ ] Previous audit CARs are closed or have documented remediation plans

### Logistics Preparation

- [ ] Audit schedule agreed with certification body
- [ ] Key personnel (CEO, ISMS Manager, Engineering Lead) available for audit days
- [ ] Meeting room or video call platform confirmed
- [ ] Wi-Fi / screen sharing access confirmed for evidence review

### Staff Briefing

- [ ] All staff briefed on audit dates and expectations
- [ ] Staff reminded of incident reporting procedure, data classification, and their role in the ISMS
- [ ] Key personnel briefed on their specific interview topics
