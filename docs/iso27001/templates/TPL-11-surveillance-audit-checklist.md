## Instructions

ISO 27001:2022 requires annual surveillance audits in Years 1 and 2 of the certification cycle, and a full recertification audit in Year 3. This checklist ensures Inovy is prepared for each surveillance visit.

Surveillance audits typically last 1–2 days and sample a subset of controls. The auditor will verify that:

1. The ISMS is still operating effectively
2. Nonconformities from the previous audit are closed
3. Continual improvement is evident
4. No significant undocumented changes have been made to the ISMS scope or controls

**Complete this checklist 8 weeks before the audit. Address any gaps immediately.**
